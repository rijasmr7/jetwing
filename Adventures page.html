WELCOME TO
JETWING SAFARI CAMP

The Home of Natta Kota
Ayubowan (May you live long)


Stretching inland from the untamed coast of the Indian Ocean, the wilderness of Yala National Park blankets the south east of Sri Lanka for several thousand acres. At the edge of the wild is a one-of-a-kind experience, bringing together unparalleled wildlife experiences and sublime cabins.


Nestled amidst golden coastal dunes just beyond the borders of Yala National Park – one of the premier locations in the world for leopards, elephants, and bears as well as a paradise for birds – our tents provide an immersive experience among the sights and sounds of the island’s remarkable fauna.


With our team of attentive butlers and passionate rangers to indulge and guide you during your stay, and the taste of authentic Sri Lankan hospitality, Jetwing Safari Camp lies poised to welcome you to the wilderness of Yala.


Naminda Rathnayake
General Manager - Jetwing Yala & Jetwing Safari Camp
