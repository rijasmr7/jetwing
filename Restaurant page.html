Mermaid hotels & club-Kalutara

The Mermaid Hotel and Club opened its doors in 1983 with a modest inventory of fifty-one rooms and only a single star rating. The express intent of the hotel developer at the time was to provide comfortable accommodation, wholesome meals and other essential facilities for the holidaymaker.

The hotel’s club concept was an addition that came during the late 1990s which saw the introduction of a well-equipped sports facility, dedicated activity pool and spa facilities among many other improvements.

Over the past twenty-eight years, constant enhancements have helped make the Mermaid Hotel and Club a highly acclaimed Sri Lanka beach resort.

The passage of time has seen the total room inventory increasing from fifty-one to a current eighty-one, and a deluxe class of accommodation being introduced with the hotel’s most recent refurbishment in 2010.

Jetwing St.Andrew's- Nuwara-eliya

High above the coastal plains of Sri Lanka, mountain clouds rest upon a blanket of tea plantations. It was in these very highlands where the colonial British retreated for cooler climes, and built empires from hill stations that became their adopted home. A timeless favourite has always been the city of Nuwara Eliya – a city so nostalgic, that it has since earned the nickname of ‘Little England’.

Set apart from Nuwara Eliya hotels, our hill country home of hospitality lives up to the city’s historical moniker in all forms. As a restored Georgian style country mansion, we invite you to step back in time and indulge in all the pleasures of upcountry luxury – as did the English of yore, but now, with a contemporary Sri Lankan touch.
